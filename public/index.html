GradType

We have trained a neural network to identify the users by their manner of typing on keyboard. The recurrent neural network learned to recognize small timing difference between keypresses to successfully identify the "typer" given 30-40 previously seen sentences.

Type few sentences below to play with it or log in with GitHub to store your identifiers for extensive testing (and further improvement of the network).
